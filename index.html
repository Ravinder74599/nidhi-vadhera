ROMANCING TARGETS

Get Closer to Selling in 7 Steps

In Romancing Targets, Nidhi Vadhera shares with you a systematic approach to understand the sales process which will empower you to embrace sales targets and achieve results without getting burnt in the process.
Using this knowledge, you’ll be able to tackle issues like stiff market competition, mental barriers, managing emotions and handling expectations by attracting, engaging and delighting your customers. This book will help you understand how the sales process can be used to sell any product/service, ensure repeat business and add to your credibility without compromising on profits in today's competitive scenario.
This book includes examples of how systematic approach can ease things out, add to individual learning curves and build strong relationships for aspiring entrepreneurs and corporate professionals.

About
Nidhi Vadhera

NIDHI VADHERA is one of those rare people who have the ability to influence without authority with her subject know how and interpersonal skills.

She is a leading sales coach, business consultant and India’s first woman author on sales, to come to the fore and unveil ground-breaking realities about sales. Her extensive research and rigorous on-ground sales experience lead her to curate and implement effective processes in human capital development and management. She consults individual contributors, business owners and organisations by helping them streamline their existing processes, optimise their resources, embrace new opportunities and overcome challenges.

She firmly believes in creating and implementing processes that are simple and effective. Her vision of teaching the value of mindset and processes across various functions in businesses, has led her to author her first book ‘Romancing Targets’, which is a descriptive representation of integral practices in the sales journey. Her professional journey began as a Sales Executive with a leading financial services organisation, where she went through her own set of struggles in achieving numbers, managing internal and external teams and developing a resilient funnel for future business. An author, an avid reader (reads and reviews over 50 books annually on her youtube channel) equips her to acquire data and industry insights for learning and development. She is a celebrated keynote speaker!

She realised the necessity to bridge gaps between perceived selling and actual selling; which encouraged me to design learning programs to simplify even the most complex challenges in sales that are relevant across industries. Today, over 4,230 entrepreneurs and individuals rely on Nidhi’s analytical approach and deep understanding of business scenarios to identify a robust action plan that leads to tangible results. She has delivered sessions to national and international clients and most are recurring clients. She has covered different industries and verticals through her sessions. She has spoken widely on improving motivation, performance, leadership, change, innovation, sales and team work. Nidhi is the founder of Sales Magnet Academy. She is on a mission is Inspire and Empower 100,000 individuals to beam with confidence when they SELL!
